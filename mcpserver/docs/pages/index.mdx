## Example Conversation

```
You: What pools are available on Ammplify?

Claude: [calls get_pools] There are 10 pools on Ammplify. The largest by TVL
is WMON/USDC (0.3% fee) with $14K TVL. Other pools include USDC/WETH,
WBTC/USDC, shMON/WMON, and CHOG/WMON.

You: Open a maker position on the WMON/USDC pool around the current price.

Claude: [calls get_pool_info] Current price is 0.0268 USDC/WMON at tick -312514.
I'll set up a range from tick -312600 to -312420 (centered, ±~0.5%).
First I need to approve both tokens...
[calls approve_token for WMON, then USDC]
[calls open_maker with compounding=true]
Position opened! Asset ID: 42. You're now earning swap fees + borrow fees.
```

## How Ammplify Works

**Makers (LPs)** deposit liquidity into concentrated tick ranges — earning swap fees from the underlying Uniswap V3 pool plus borrow fees from Takers who borrow their liquidity. Think of it as writing covered calls and getting paid for it.

**The protocol manages everything** through a binary tree structure that handles fee distribution, auto-compounding, and liquidity redistribution automatically.
